FAQ

Is it safe for my account? +
InstaErase acts like you doing it yourself. It simply does the clicking for you.
Do you read my messages? +
Never. InstaErase runs locally in your browser. We do not store your DMs, comments, or photos on external servers.
Do I need to share my password? +
No. You log into Instagram normally in your browser. InstaErase works on your normal web session. There is no password handoff to us.
What can’t be deleted? +
InstaErase can only remove things you can remove yourself on Instagram web. If Instagram does not allow it, the extension cannot either.
